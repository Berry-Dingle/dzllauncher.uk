Privacy

Short version: DZLL is local-first and doesn’t run a tracking backend.

What DZLL stores

  • Local settings (e.g. launch options, preferences).
  • Local favourites / last played history (on your machine).

What DZLL does not collect

  • No analytics tracking.
  • No telemetry uploads.
  • No personal data is sent to a DZLL server (there isn’t one).

Discord Rich Presence

If enabled, DZLL updates your Discord activity using Discord’s local RPC. Use Privacy Mode to hide server details.

This page is informational and may be updated as DZLL evolves.
