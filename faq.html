FAQ

Quick answers to the stuff that comes up most.

Does DZLL need SteamCMD?

No. DZLL can launch DayZ without SteamCMD mod handling. SteamCMD is only for optional mod install/update/verify.

Why do I sometimes get Steam Guard prompts?

SteamCMD login can trigger Steam Guard. If you enable auto-update/verify, SteamCMD may run more often.

What does “Privacy Mode” do?

When you choose “On Server” detail level in Discord Rich Presence, Privacy Mode hides server details while still showing you’re playing DayZ.

Is DZLL affiliated with Bohemia Interactive?

No. DZLL is an unofficial community launcher.
